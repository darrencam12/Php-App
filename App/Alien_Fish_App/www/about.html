Spot the Alien Fish

Spot the Alien Fish is a citizen science campaign through which the sea users and the public in general are encouraged to contribute information on the arrival of new (alien) fish species to Maltese waters and how these fish species are spreading over time in our waters.

The campaign is coordinated by Prof. Alan Deidun from the Department of Geosciences at the University of Malta and builds on the success of the Spot the Jellyfish citizen science campaign. The campaign is supported by the IOI, the University of Malta, the Department of Fisheries and ERA.

Through the Spot the Alien Fish, everyone can act as a marine biologist by helping scientists in their day-to-day work, especially since more and more marine alien species are reaching our waters.
